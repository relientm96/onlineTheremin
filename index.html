Note:
- Move your left wrist (green) up and down to change current note.
- Move your right wrist (blue) up and down to change current note octave.
- Red Keypoints are other keypoints detected on your body (such as nose, eyes ...).
- Minimum Threshold Score determines minimum score value to consider it as a keypoint.
--> Higher means higher accuracy but less tolerance for keypoint detection.
--> Lower means lower accuracy but higher tolerance for keypoint detection.
